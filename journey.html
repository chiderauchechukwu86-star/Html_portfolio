My journey into software development began with a growing interest in technology, creativity, and the way modern digital products influence everyday life. What started as curiosity quickly became a serious passion for building websites and understanding how professional user interfaces are designed and developed.

Joining GMT Software School has played an important role in helping me build a solid foundation in frontend development. Through practical lessons, coding exercises, and hands-on projects, I have been learning how to structure websites properly using HTML and style them professionally with CSS.

Throughout this learning process, I have also discovered the importance of design systems, spacing, typography, responsiveness, animations, and user experience. Coming from a graphics design background, I naturally enjoy creating interfaces that feel modern, clean, visually balanced, and attractive to users.

Every stage of this journey has helped me become more disciplined, patient, and creative. Although I am still growing as a developer, I constantly challenge myself to improve by building projects, experimenting with modern UI styles, and learning new techniques that can make my work stand out.

My long-term vision is to grow into a professional developer capable of building premium digital experiences for brands, businesses, startups, and individuals globally. I am excited about the future and fully committed to becoming better every single day.
